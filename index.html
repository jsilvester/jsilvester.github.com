About

My name is James Silvester, I am not a professional photographer, just someone who enjoys photography as an escape. Born in England, I lived in Canada for 8 years and now I live in Uppsala, Sweden. I shoot both digital and film. From this site you can select to view my photography blog where I post mini-reviews/articles or you check out my photography on my social media sites.
