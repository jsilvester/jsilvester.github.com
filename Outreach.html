I am an avid volunteer at the Queen’s observatory, from 2008 to 2009  I took the role of observatory co-coordinator which required me to be responsible for all outreach activities at the observatory.

I am very fond of producing astronomy content for public education, having contributed to Astronomy Now magazine in the UK and given public lectures both in the UK and Canada.

During 2008-2009 I hosted and produced an astronomy radio show on CFRC called Astrarium, which was rebroadcast on Astronomy.FM and can also be found on iTunes
